Resume Guides

Start with these in‑depth guides to craft concise, metrics‑driven bullets for your role.

These guides collect practical, role‑specific patterns, action verbs, and examples to help you write resume bullets that hiring managers can evaluate quickly. Each guide contains concrete before/after examples, impact formulas, and recommendations on how to choose metrics and scope that reflect your real contribution. Use the guides alongside the Bullet Improver tool to iterate and refine your bullets.

We recommend reading the step‑by‑step guide first if you're new to writing quantified bullets, then using the role-specific examples to find verbs and formats that match your experience. The guides are intentionally concise so you can apply patterns quickly; long-form content such as templates and deep cheat sheets are available on the Resources page.
