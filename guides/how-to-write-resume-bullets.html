How to Write Resume Bullets (Step‑by‑Step)

Use this 4‑part formula to turn vague tasks into compelling achievements.

Resume.io Templates Rezi

The 4‑Part Formula

  1. Action Verb — Led, Engineered, Optimized, Launched, Automated
  2. What — feature/system/project with a brief scope
  3. How — tools, techniques, team, constraints, scale
  4. Impact — outcome with a metric and timeframe

Before → After Examples

Before: Worked on checkout page

After: Optimized checkout rendering and reduced API calls, increasing conversion +19% in 6 weeks.

Before: Responsible for backend performance

After: Refactored hot paths and introduced Redis caching, cutting p99 latency −42%.

Tips

  • Prefer past tense and avoid first‑person pronouns.
  • Include at least one number: %, ms, $, users, regions, timeframe.
  • Trim filler (very, basically) and avoid hype; be specific.
  • Group bullets by impact; lead with your strongest results.
