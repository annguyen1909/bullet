About Bullet Improver

A tiny tool to help you turn vague tasks into measurable, resume-ready achievements.

Bullet Improver was created to help job seekers quickly rewrite resume bullets so they highlight ownership, scale, and impact. The site offers short, practical guidance and a simple text tool that suggests three styled alternatives for a single bullet. Our goal is to make it faster to craft clear, truthful, and metrics-focused lines that hiring managers and recruiters can evaluate efficiently.

We strive to keep the product privacy-first: inputs are only used to generate suggestions and are not retained long-term. For details on data handling and analytics, see the Privacy Policy.
