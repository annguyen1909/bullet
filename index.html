Resume Bullet Improver

Paste a resume bullet, pick a style, and get 3 improved versions.

Bullet Improver is a small, privacy‑conscious tool that helps you rewrite resume bullets into clearer, metrics‑focused lines. Paste a single bullet into the box, choose a style, and get three curated variations you can copy into your resume or LinkedIn.

We do not retain your inputs long‑term. For details about data handling and cookies, see our Privacy Policy. The quality of suggestions improves when you include scope, tools, and a timeframe in your original bullet.
